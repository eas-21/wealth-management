Recyclable Water-Resistant Roof Tiles

Recycled Polymer Sand Composite material for making 100% Recyclable Water-Resistant Roof Tiles, Paver Blocks & Parking Tiles. These tiles provide both vertical and horizontal airflow, significantly improving indoor climate control. This aspect of the design further enhances their effectiveness in managing heat, making them a practical and sustainable choice for roofing in areas with high temperatures.
